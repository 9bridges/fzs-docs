### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment:** The site largely avoids AI-generated tells. No gradient text, glassmorphism, numbered section markers, oversized radius, sketchy SVGs, or stripe backgrounds. Three borderline violations: hero-metric stats bar (P0), card grid repetition across 3 homepage sections, and ghost-card violation on .whatWeDoCard:hover (1px border + 20px blur shadow).

**Deterministic scan:** detect.mjs found 18 advisory issues: 7× border-radius: 10px outside DESIGN.md scale, 11× undocumented colors (rgba dark mode shadows). All advisory — intentional choices not captured in tokens.

### Overall Impression

Punches above its weight. Brand crimson as structural color, Outfit/Noto Sans SC typography, specificity-driven copy all communicate "real engineering." Biggest opportunity: layout variety — 3 consecutive card grids on homepage, 6 card patterns total.

### What's Working

1. **Brand Crimson as Structural Color**: oklch(40% 0.16 25) in earned roles only. Crimson-tinted shadows integrate depth into palette.
2. **Specificity-Driven Copy**: Every claim backed by named system or concrete number. DBAs recognize authentic engineering communication.
3. **Scroll-Reveal with Restraint**: Blur entrance fits precision brand. prefers-reduced-motion correct.

2. **[P0] Card Grid Repetition Across 3 Homepage Sections** — WhatWeDo, Industries, Explore are near-identical card grids. Fix: Vary at least one section layout. Command: $impeccable layout src/pages/index.js
3. **[P1] Database Chip Wall on /product Has No Categorization** — 15 undifferentiated pills. Fix: Group into categories with visual separators. Command: $impeccable layout src/pages/product.js
4. **[P1] border-radius: 10px Drift from DESIGN.md Scale** — 7 instances across all pages. Scale defines 4/6/8/12/999 — 10px isn't there. Fix: Add md2: "10px" or consolidate. Command: $impeccable document
5. **[P1] Ghost-Card Violation on .whatWeDoCard:hover** — border-color change + fzs-shadow-md (blur 20px). Fix: Drop shadow on hover. Command: $impeccable polish src/pages/index.module.css
6. **[P2] --ifm-color-emphasis-600 Contrast is Borderline** — ~4.1:1 against warm ground, below WCAG AA 4.5:1. Fix: Bump to emphasis-700/800. Command: $impeccable audit src/pages

### Persona Red Flags

**Jordan (First-Timer):** No "Start here" guided path. FAQ is 5 scroll-sections deep. "亚秒级" has no context for newcomers.

**Riley (Stress Tester):** content-visibility: auto used inconsistently. QR failure mode: alt text only, no fallback UI.

**Casey (Distracted Mobile User):** hideOnScroll is severe mobile friction. Database chips require significant vertical scrolling at 375px. Case cards stack to 400-500px each.

**IT Director (Project-Specific):** No compliance badges (等保, ISO 27001). No SLA language. WeChat QR as primary contact reads casual. No deployment topology diagrams on product page. No licensing model visible.

### Minor Observations

1. .hero radial-gradient identical across all 5 pages — decorative effort without payoff.
2. Font loading is render-blocking via @import. Consider font-display: swap.
3. Dark mode QR inversion (filter: invert(1)) is aggressive — may produce unpredictable results with colored backgrounds.
4. About page has two TODO placeholders — reads as unfinished.

### Questions to Consider

1. Would a compliance badge strip (等保三级, ISO 27001) above the fold change conversion more than any design refinement?
2. Is the marketing/docs visual split too sharp? Should docs inherit more marketing design language?
3. What does the WeChat link preview look like when sharing /product? og:image is 167×48, not 1200×630.
